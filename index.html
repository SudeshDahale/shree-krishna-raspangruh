Our Heritage

Pure Devotion

Named in reverence to Lord Krishna — the embodiment of purity and abundance — Shree Krishna Raspangure has served Nashik for over fifteen years with one unwavering promise: nothing but freshness.

Every fruit is sourced at peak ripeness, pressed the same morning, chilled to crystalline perfection, and served with the warmth of tradition. No syrups. No concentrates. No compromises.

"The finest juice tastes exactly like the fruit — nothing added, nothing lost."
